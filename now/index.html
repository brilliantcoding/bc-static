Now

Currently I’m in San Francisco. Currently I’m focused on making this blog awesome. And currently it is being migrated to a version 2 static site generator. Most likely this will be Metalsmith, but I’ve used Pelican before for some projects so I will be considering that as well.

So the blog caching issues are finally gone. Running in a purely cached mode is just too much of a headache. I’m going to be migrating the site content over to a static site generator and get off of WordPress. Without a doubt WordPress is awesome to get a blog up and running quickly. But eventually as you after you figure out the fiddly bits like template design, colors, CSS, comments, etc etc. I find that I want to automate more and more of the site, and there just are limits to what can be done with WordPress.
