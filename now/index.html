Welcome 2020!

The next decade should be *much* more exciting for this site.  I started this about 5 years ago to have an alternate publishing platform.  After a few iterations it runs with little to no effort.  Here’s a quick rundown of what is coming technically: subscribe to content (the site emails the blog post), fuller integration with Twitter for comments/discussions, no tracking/cookies (unless you login to subscribe in which case full GDPR/CPA support).

Also I just made some minor tweaks with a new WordPress release.  Also I noticed that I accidentally changed the whole permalink structure of the blog!  Reverting it back now, this is why static publishing is so nice, the old urls never went away.  I caught the error when the new urls started to index.
